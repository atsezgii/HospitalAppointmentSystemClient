Great doctor if you need your family member to get effective immediate assistance, emergency treatment or a simple consultation.

The appointment system is an online platform that allows patients to schedule, view, and manage their appointments with doctors at our hospital.

You can register by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling out the registration form with your personal details.

To schedule an appointment, log in to your account, select a doctor and an available time slot, and confirm your appointment.

If no time slots are available, you can choose a different doctor or date.
